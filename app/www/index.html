USER AGREEMENTS

IASTracker is a project to obtain the geolocation of the main Invasive Alien Species (IAS) of European regions. This project is based in collecting IAS observations with citizen’s collaboration. Therefore a dataset of the introduction or presence of IAS is fed with voluntaries collaboration using IASTracker App. To participate in IASTracker project you must be an adult or participate under adult supervision.

Before installing IASTracker App you must know IASTracker privacy statements you must agree with the following terms:
  • You agree in share your observations, photos and comments as open data. This data can be reused without restrictions. It means that you grants IASTracker project a world-wide, royalty-free, non-exclusive, sublicensable licence to use, reproduce, modify and make derivate works, adapt, publish, translate, distribute, lend and rent and sub-license rights.
  • You warrant that your data does not libel anyone, invade anyone’s privacy, infringe anyone’s copyright, or otherwise does not violate the law or anyone's rights.
  • You agree to indemnify the IASTracker project members and co-workers if any third party takes action against them in relation to the material that you submit.

PERSONAL INFORMATION PUBLISHED ON IASTracker WEBSITE AND APPLICATIONS

All personal information displayed on the IASTracker website or IASTracker applications is subject to the following Specific Privacy Statement, accepted by the data subject prior to the collection of his/her personal data, and is published with his/her unambiguous consent.

SPECIFIC PRIVACY STATEMENT

1. Description

IASTRacker project gathers data shared by voluntaries and makes it available as open data. The data collected by voluntaries are Invasive Alien Species (IAS) observations, this information can be used by research community, environmental authorities and also it is given to the general public.

As this processing collects personal data, Spanish Law 15/1999 of December 13 (http://www.boe.es/diario_boe/txt.php?id=BOE-A-1999-23750) on the Protection of Personal Data is applicable.

2. What personal information do we collect, what is the legal basis, for what purpose and through which technical means?

Identification Data:

The personal data collected and further processed may include: First name, last name, email address, profile photo, and type of voluntary.

Purpose of processing:

The purpose of the processing of personal data for IASTracker is to provide a feedback in the IAS observations validation procedure and to optimize this validation management.

Technical Information:

The user data are collected through web forms and stored inside a SQL database.

3. How do we protect and safeguard your information?

Bearing in mind the important requirement of protection and confidentiality for personal data, all the technical and organisational measures needed for safeguarding the security of the data have been taken, as required by current regulations on security measures for files containing personal data.

 4. How can you verify, modify or delete your information?

Registered users have direct password-protected web access to their profile and are able to update it. They can exercise, at any time, their right to access, rectify, cancel and oppose any data held on under the terms and provisions set out in current legislation on data protection by sending an email to info@iastracker.ic5team.org, indicating in the Subject: “LOPD Rights Protection.

5. Contact Information

Should you have any queries concerning the processing of your personal data, please address
them to info@iastracker.ic5team.org
